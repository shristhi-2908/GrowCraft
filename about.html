About Us

Who We Are — GrowCraft

GrowCraft is a service-based platform designed to help businesses grow their online presence while also empowering students and contributors to gain hands-on experience in real-world projects. We pair client work with mentorship-driven contributor flows so learning happens by doing.

This project is proudly part of GirlScript Summer of Code 2025 (GSSoC'25) — a nationwide open-source program where contributors collaborate, learn, and build impactful solutions.
